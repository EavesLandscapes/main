About

Small crew. High standards.

Eaves Landscapes is a locally owned and operated landscaping company serving Zionsville, Westfield, and Whitestown. We keep our crew small on purpose — it means every property gets personal attention and the owner is on-site for every project.

We believe your outdoor space should reflect the same care you put into your home. That means quality materials, honest communication, and work we're proud to stand behind. No cutting corners, no surprise invoices, no disappearing contractors.

Whether you need a full landscape redesign or dependable weekly mowing, we treat your property like it's our own.
